broodROM Jellybean Manifest

Version: Release 2

You may clone broodROM JB manifest for educational purposes only. Releasing broodROM JB is not allowed, unless you ask me permission to port it to an other device

broodplank.net

Download:

  • mkdir ~/broodROM
  • cd ~/broodROM
  • repo init -u https://github.com/broodplank/android -b jellybean
  • repo sync -j16 (for 100mbit)

Initialize:

  • . build/envsetup.sh
  • lunch > choose full_ariesve-userdebug

Build:

  • make (-k) -j#
